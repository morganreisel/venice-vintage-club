What Is This?

"A place where the racks have stories, the walls are for sale, and everyone's invited."

Venice Vintage Club is a curated vintage experience out of Venice, California. Part pop-up shop, part community space, part creative archive. Think basement living room meets flea market meets art gallery.

We host pop-up weekends where you can dig through racks, find one-of-a-kind pieces, and be part of something that feels like the swap meets and co-ops that used to bring people together — just on a smaller, more personal scale.

Everything on the walls is for sale. Everything on the racks has been lived in. Patina signs, band tees, collectibles, art — if it's in the space, it's part of the collection. This is vintage with a pulse.
