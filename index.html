The Club

What Is This?

Venice Vintage Club is a curated vintage experience out of Venice, California. Part pop-up shop, part community space, part creative archive. We sell hand-picked vintage clothing, collectibles, patina signs, wall art, and anything with a story worth telling.

Think basement living room meets flea market meets art gallery. We host pop-up weekends where you can come dig through racks, find one-of-a-kind pieces, and be part of something that feels like the swap meets and co-ops that used to bring people together — just on a smaller, more personal scale.

Everything on the walls is for sale. Everything on the racks has been lived in. This is vintage with a pulse.
