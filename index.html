About VVC

Meet Our Founder, Morgan

Venice Vintage Club started the way the best things do — out of a garage, fueled by obsession. Morgan Reisel grew up thrifting, collecting, and falling in love with pieces that had a past. VVC is her way of sharing that with Venice.

Every item is handpicked. Every rack is curated. No algorithms, no mass production — just one person's eye for the stuff that tells a story. From 90s denim and vintage signage to band tees and one-of-ones, if it made the cut, it's because Morgan believed in it.

VVC isn't a store. It's a club. A community of people who care about where things come from and the lives they've lived. Pull up, dig through the racks, and find something worth keeping.
