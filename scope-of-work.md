## The Drop — Stripe Payment Links (Light Touch)

Each item in the curated collection (21 items) gets a photo, caption, and a direct "Buy" link to a Stripe checkout URL created by the client. No cart, no sizing, no inventory management. This is included as part of the initial site build.

If the client wants a full shop experience (product pages, cart, size selectors, inventory tracking), that is a separate project quoted as full e-commerce above.

---

## Design Assets

Client owns all brand assets created for the project. Flat logo files (PNG) will be provided. Animated assets (spinning globe, blinking sign, etc.) were built as part of the website and live on the site — animated exports (GIFs, video files) are not included.

---

## How It Works

1. Client submits a request (text, email, or DM).
2. If it falls within the retainer, it gets done within 3–5 business days.
3. If it falls outside the retainer, a quote is provided before any work starts.

---

## What the Client Covers

- Domain registration and renewal
- Hosting / platform fees
- Any third-party service subscriptions (Formspree, Shopify, etc.)
- All business operations, finances, and payment processing (in-person and online)
- Point-of-sale setup and management for live events
- Pricing, inventory, and sales tracking

Website support does not include business operations, financial setup, or event logistics. Any involvement beyond the website would be scoped as a separate arrangement.
